🔬 Silver-Ion Technology

The science behind VoltSwap's performance advantage

6 Key Techniques
1
Silver-Ion Electrode Coating
Alternating Ag⁺ + TCA layers ensure uniform Li deposition. Prevents dendrite formation entirely.
2
Ag-C Composite Anode
Samsung's silver-carbon layer enables 500 Wh/kg energy density — nearly 2x standard Li-ion (270 Wh/kg).
3
Oxide Solid Electrolyte
Replaces flammable liquid electrolyte. Non-flammable, thermally stable, zero fire risk.
4
In-Situ Nanoparticle Formation
During charging, Ag⁺ → Ag nanoparticles that guide lithium atoms to deposit evenly.
5
TCA Protective Matrix
Trithioisocyanuric acid forms a flexible layer preventing cracking. 2,000+ hours stable operation in labs.
6
Room-Temp Solution Process
No high-temp synthesis needed. Simple room-temperature coating — scalable Indian manufacturing.

Key Risks & Mitigation
⚠️ Silver-ion cost is high ▼
Start with Li-ion packs, migrate to silver-ion as procurement scales. Use hybrid fleet: silver-ion for premium/fleet, Li-ion for pilot/budget tier. Lock in Korean supplier partnerships early for volume pricing.
⚠️ Battery standardization issue ▼
Focus on one vehicle type first (e-rickshaw 48V or scooter 72V). Build proprietary adapter system for top 5 Jaipur vehicle models. Compatibility = our software moat.
⚠️ Battery Smart / Indofast competition ▼
Go where they are weak: Tier-2 Jaipur neighborhoods. Differentiate on silver-ion tech and battery performance guarantee. Build partner density faster — network effects protect us.
⚠️ Partner churn if swap volume low ▼
Only onboard partners in validated high-traffic zones. Give minimum swap guarantees in early contracts. Provide driver acquisition support. Track station performance weekly.
